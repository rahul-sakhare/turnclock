    How to use
    1. Set Up — choose the number of players and minutes per turn, then press Set Up.
    2. Players — add names and pick a color for each player (colors are pre-assigned; change them anytime).
    3. Roll — spin the slot machine to randomly pick who goes first. Locks once the game starts.
    4. Start — begin the countdown. The screen pulses blue for 5 seconds at each new turn, flashes red in the last 10 seconds, and turns solid red counting negative when time runs out.
    5. Pass the turn — tap the active player's wedge (or Next Player) when they finish. Their time stops and the next player's countdown begins.
    6. Track time — the side table shows each player's cumulative time and share of the total. Reset wipes everything after confirmation.
    7. Scores — at the end of the game, type each player's final score into the boxes in the table and press Submit Scores to see the time-adjusted ranking and podium.
    8. Time-adjusted formula — Adjusted = Score × (2 × MeanTime) ÷ (YourTime + MeanTime). Playing at exactly average time keeps your raw score; playing faster boosts it (capped at ×2), playing slower trims it — so a tiny score can't win on speed alone.
